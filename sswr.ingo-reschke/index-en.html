SSWR Pregnancy Weeks Calculator - App

Description

Are you pregnant? What week of pregnancy are you in? The Pregnancy Weeks Calculator (SSWR) is designed for pregnant women and medical professionals who want to calculate the exact pregnancy week based on the estimated due date (E.D.). In addition to the week calculation, you will find the entry in the maternity record (corresponding to the completed week plus days of the current week) and an indication of how many days are left until the E.D. or how many days you have been pregnant.
