Privacy Policy for Android App SSWR

Thank you for using the SSWR app. We value the protection of your privacy and personal data. In this privacy policy, we will inform you about how we handle your data.

1. Collection and Processing of User Data:

  • SSWR does not collect or process any personal user data.
  • However, the app uses the AdMob advertising network by Google to display personalized ads. AdMob may collect and process anonymous device information and usage statistics to provide relevant advertising. These data are not linked to personally identifiable information.

2. Use of Cookies:

  • SSWR does not use cookies for the collection of user data.

3. Data Disclosure:

  • Since no user data is collected or processed, we do not disclose any data to third parties.

4. Data Security:

  • We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against loss, misuse, or unauthorized access. However, please note that no transmission of data over the internet is completely secure, and therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy:

  • We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time. We will inform you about significant changes through relevant notifications within the app.

6. Contact:

  • If you have any questions or concerns regarding the privacy policy, you can reach us at kontakt[at]sswr.ingo-reschke.de.
